Our Story

Building Trust, One Floor at a Time

Ale Floors began with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and businesses in Florida with the highest quality floor covering solutions. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in epoxy flooring across Orlando, Tampa, and Daytona Beach.

With over 9 years of experience and more than 1533 completed projects, we've built our reputation on quality workmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service. Every member of our team is trained, certified, and passionate about transforming ordinary floors into extraordinary spaces.
